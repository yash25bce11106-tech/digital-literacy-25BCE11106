## Stay Safe Online Checklist

- Always verify website URLs before entering personal information  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links in emails or messages  
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible  
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone  
- Keep your devices and apps updated regularly  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions  
- Log out from accounts on shared devices  

## UPI / Financial Safety

- Always verify the UPI ID or recipient name before sending money  
- Never approve unknown payment requests or collect requests  

## Report Cybercrime

- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
